Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat until foaming.
  2. Cook the chorizo in a non-stick frying pan over high heat, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes or until crisp.
  3. Stir in the chorizo and parsley. Season with pepper.
